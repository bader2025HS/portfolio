My Work

Interactive Human Security Twine Game

Designed an interactive digital experience to teach key Human Security concepts—including the 7 Securities, Responsibility to Protect (R2P), and radicalization—through branching narratives, embedded quizzes, and visual storytelling. The project aimed to make complex policy frameworks accessible and engaging for a broader audience.

Asylum Research and Legal Support for LGBT+ Refugees

Supported asylum applicants by drafting case briefs and conducting contextual research for OFPRA and CNDA hearings. Authored a 70-page report on the situation of LGBT+ individuals in the DRC, Cameroon, Senegal, and Côte d’Ivoire, used to support claims and inform legal strategy.

Research: French ODA Allocation in West Africa

Studied how France allocates Official Development Assistance (ODA) across West African countries, using data from 2003 to 2023. The research analyzed trends, priorities, and underlying political and economic logics guiding aid distribution, offering insights into France’s development strategy and regional engagement.
